About

Actress and singer Shar Jackson is most famously known as Kevin Federline‘s pre-Britney Spears flame. Prior to dating and having two children with Federline, Jackson made a name for herself playing Niecy Jackson on UPN’s hit show Moesha.

Jackson has also made an impression in the music world as well; in 2007 she was named the winner of Celebrity Rap Superstar with her song, “Let it Blow.” Jackson was also a member of the now-defunct group Mpulz which was featured on the Princess Diaries soundtrack.

She’s also been linked to director Quentin Taratino.
