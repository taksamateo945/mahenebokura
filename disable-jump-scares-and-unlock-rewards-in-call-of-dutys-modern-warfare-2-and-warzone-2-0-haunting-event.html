Gamers have been enjoying The Haunting event in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 and Warzone 2.0 in preparation for Halloween. Jump scares have been added to the game, though, so not everyone is enjoying themselves immensely. For a lot of players, these unexpected shocks can be rather unpleasant. However, there is good news: turning them off is simple. This article explains how to turn off jump scares during The Haunting event and gives advice on how to avoid getting spooked when playing video games. Disable Jump Scares and Unlock Rewards in Call of Duty’s Modern Warfare 2 and Warzone 2.0 Haunting Event

Disabling Jump Scares in Warzone 2.0 and Modern Warfare 2

To Disable jump scares in Call of Duty, follow these simple steps:

  • Boot Up MW2 or Warzone: Start by launching either Modern Warfare 2 or Warzone 2.0.
  • Navigate to the Battle Pass Menu: Once you’re in the game, go to the Battle Pass menu.
  • Deactivate Jump Scares: Press the L2/LT button to turn off the Battle Pass jump scares.
  • Enjoy a Jump-Scares-Free Experience: You’ve successfully disabled jump scares in the Battle Pass.

Even if you aren’t easily frightened by jump scares, it’s worth noting that their volume can be quite loud, potentially disrupting your gameplay. If you play with the volume turned up, consider taking these precautions:

  • Adjust the Game’s Volume: Lower the in-game volume if necessary.
  • Wear Headphones: Using headphones will allow you to hear in-game sounds more clearly.
  • Stay Alert: Pay attention to your surroundings and try to anticipate when jump scares might occur.

COD Warfare 2 and Warzone 2.0: The Haunting Event 

The Haunting event is available in both Modern Warfare 2 and Call of Duty: Warzone 2.0. This event, known as the “Soul Capture Event,” is a spooky twist on the popular Trophy Hunt Event from Season 03. Here’s what you need to know:

  • Collect Souls: Whenever a player is eliminated, they leave behind a Soul. These Souls are automatically added to your total in all game modes, eliminating the need to visit a Buy Station in Call of Duty: Warzone or DMZ.
  • Trade Souls for Rewards: You can exchange collected Souls for items listed in the Events Tab.

Some of the rewards you can obtain during The Haunting event include:

  • Gone Batty (Weapon Sticker)
  • Skullified (Calling Card)
  • Bit Corny (Charm)
  • Creature Copter (Vehicle Skin)
  • Alien Death Ray (Weapon Blueprint)
  • Join Me (Loading Screen)
  • Bad Luck? (Charm)
  • Carved Up (Weapon Sticker)
  • Violent End (Weapon Blueprint)
  • XP Tokens
  • Tier Skips
  • And more, with additional rewards to be unveiled throughout The Haunting.

Jump scares aren’t for everyone, so you don’t have to put up with them. You can enjoy The Haunting event in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 and Warzone 2.0 without having to deal with those unexpected shocks if you follow the procedures to disable them. Additionally, benefit from the Soul Capture Event to win some exciting prizes.
