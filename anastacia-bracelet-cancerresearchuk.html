Anastacia launches diamond bracelet in support of Cancer Research UK

August 29, 2024
blog

Pop powerhouse Anastacia has released a limited edition diamond bracelet in support of Cancer Research UK.

Teaming up with Arctic Circle Diamonds, the stunning bracelet is limited to a production of 100 pieces. Anastacia launched the bracelet last night (October 26), at a star studded event which was attended by the likes of her Strictly Come Dancing friends Laura Whitmore, Ore Oduba, and Rob Rinder.

Proceeds from the bracelet will go to Cancer Research UK in order to fund life-saving research specifically into breast cancer. Having overcome breast cancer twice, the cause is extremely close to Anastacia’s heart. Speaking at the event, she said: “After beating breast cancer twice, I’m really passionate about trying to do something to help change the odds for others who are affected by this terrible disease. If we all dig deep and donate we can raise vital funds to help improve survival rates.”

Anastacia also revealed that she visited the Francis Crick Institute earlier this week in order to further educate herself in the steps being taken to develop research in a hope to cure cancer.

If you want to get your hands on one of these exclusive bracelets, they are available to buy here for £299.

In addition to releasing the collaborative bracelet, 2017 has been an incredibly busy year for Anastacia. The songstress released her seventh studio album, Evolution, and completed her worldwide Ultimate Collection tour. She will also be embarking on a European tour in 2018.
