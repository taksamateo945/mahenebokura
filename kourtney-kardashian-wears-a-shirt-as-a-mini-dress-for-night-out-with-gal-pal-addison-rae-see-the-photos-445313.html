Kourtney Kardashian Wears A Shirt As A Mini Dress For Night Out With Gal Pal Addison Rae See The

May 15, 2024
blog

Kourtney Kardashian wore a shirt as a mini dress while out on the town with gal pal Addison Rae and the look is being talked about! Kourtney is revered for her fashion style and the Poosh founder certainly had her best looks on display as she was seen with her much younger BFF. Photos of Kourtney and Addison are making social media rounds and people are commenting!

Kourtney Kardashian wore the Ruffled Neckline, Long-Line Shirt by Bottega Veneta as a mini-dress and paired it with heels from Manolo Blahnik. The blouse is made from a blend of acetate and viscose that gives the shirt a polished sheen. The shirt’s front placard hosts ruffles as well as a cutout section for a striking look. The blouse features button cuffs and a fitted silhouette. The Ruffled Neckline, Long-Line Shirt reached the tops of Kourtney’s thighs and showed off her toned legs and retails for approximately $1360.

The Manolo Blahnik shoes that Kourtney wore are called the Leva Sandals. The strappy footwear is in high demand and quickly sells out of stock. Kourtney wore the shoes in the color black.

Kourtney carried a Bottega Veneta purse with her outfit. It is called the Pouch Bag and retails for about $2400.
