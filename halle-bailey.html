Career

Halle Bailey and her sister began a YouTube channel together, where they shared covers of music they enjoyed. The sisters released a cover of Beyoncé’s “Pretty Hurts” on December 22, 2013, which was a watershed moment in their careers.

Beyoncé was drawn to the cover once it went viral. Beyoncé shared their video on her Instagram, and it quickly went viral. When they appeared on The Ellen Show in April 2012, the team made their talk show debut. In December 2012, Radio Disney named them the winners of the fifth season of The Next Big Thing. As a result, they had a surprise appearance in the Disney sitcom Austin & Ally in September 2013, performing the song “Unstoppable.”

The EP Sugar Symphony, released under Parkwood on April 29, 2016, was Chloe x Halle’s professional debut. Chloe x Halle released their mixtape The Two of Us in the spring of 2017, which featured new music primarily written and produced by the pair.

Halle Bailey was cast as Princess Ariel in Disney’s planned live-action adaptation of The Little Mermaid, directed by Rob Marshall, in July 2019. She will also record and perform the film’s soundtrack. The pair released the single “Do It” and announced their second album, Ungodly Hour, on May 14, 2020. The following day, the second single, “Forgive Me,” was launched, along with a video.

Ungodly Hour was released on June 12, 2020, to critical acclaim. With 24,000 units sold, the album debuted at number 16 on the Billboard 200 chart. In June 2020, “Do It” debuted at number 83 on the Billboard Hot 100, marking its first appearance on the chart. A music video for the album’s title track, “Ungodly Hour,” was released on February 24, 2021.
