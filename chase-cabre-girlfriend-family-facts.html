Achievements At Young Age

Chase Cabre started racing motocross at the age of four at a local track in his hometown Dade City, Florida. He won his first-ever race at 600 Micro Sprints. Afterward, he went on to win 12 of 24 races.

Later, Cabre made a move to 360 Sprint Cars and then quickly transferred to running pavement Late Model Stocks at Hickory Motor Speedway.

In 2016, he applied for the NASCAR Drive for Diversity Program with Rev Racing. He was accepted to the combine and then was then asked to be a driver for the 2017 season in the NASCAR K&N Pro Series East.


Chase finished the 2019 season with 2 wins, 4 poles, & 7 top 5’s in the NASCAR K&N Pro Series. In 2020, he finished 4th at The Tulsa Shootout and 4th in the Non wing World Championship.
