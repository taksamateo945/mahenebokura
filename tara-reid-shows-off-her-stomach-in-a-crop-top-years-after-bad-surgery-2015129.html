Tara Reid Shows Off Her Stomach in a Crop Top Years After Bad Surgery

March 20, 2024
blog

Dare to bare! Tara Reid looked happy and carefree as she showed off her taut tummy at a charity event in Santa Monica, Calif., on Saturday, Sept. 12.

The Sharknado star wore a plain white tank top, snug black leather jeans, and a camo-colored trucker hat to support The Heroes Project at the beachside fete.

Reid, 39, famously received two botched plastic surgery procedures back in 2004 — including breast implants and a body contouring procedure gone bad.

“My stomach became the most ripply, bulgy thing,” she told Us back in 2006 of the embarrassing surgical snafu. “I had a hernia, this huge bump next to my bellybutton. As a result, I couldn’t wear a bikini. I lost a lot of work.”

Earlier this year, however, Reid rang in the New Year in her birthday suit, causing a stir on social media after she posted a shot of herself nude in a hammock in Tulum, Mexico.

Then, a few days later, the former American Pie actress posted shots of herself in a skimpy bikini taking a dip inside a cave while on her Mexican getaway.
