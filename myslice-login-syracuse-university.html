MySlice Login Syracuse University

MySlice is the official student portal of Syracuse University. MySlice is used to access student information, grades, and other resources.

MySlice is also used to register for classes, view your financial aid information, track your academic progress and more.

MySlice Register & Sign Up

  • Go to the MySlice site and click Register.
  • Enter your SUID and password, then click Log In.
  • If you have not yet provided your SSN or a tax ID number (TIN), enter it now in the appropriate field, then click Save Changes.

Syracuse University New Student Registration

You will be able to register for classes, add/drop a course and see your schedule once the registration process is complete. You can access the registration website by visiting the Student Center tab on mySlice or by clicking here: [mySlice] (http://www.syr.edu/online/.cblibrary/lib-web/studentcenter/?resource=resourcestitle&1).
